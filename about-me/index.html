Hi there! Nice to meet you. My name is Nor, pronounce Naw, I'm a Product Designer in the San Francisco Bay area. I enjoy creating useful apps for people to make their lives easier and better. I also make illustrations and I write a blog about design, art and programming.

I am trained in both design and programming of interactive media. I’ve published many award winning projects for great companies like Encyclopædia Britannica, Sears, and Barnes & Noble College just to name a few. In my spare time, I write children's books such as Xieng Mieng and Kiwi the Green Koala. I also run Sahtu Press, Inc., a nonprofit publishing company with the mission to promote Lao literature to the world.
