About the Project

This was my second iOS application. I created it to help my daughter get over her fear of the dark. I also wanted a product for my characters. I created Kiwi the Green Koala to teach children about friendship, problem solving and helping others. I'm planning to write a story for how the flashlight was created by Kiwi and his friends.

This app is not only a flashlight but it is also an alarm and nightlight.
