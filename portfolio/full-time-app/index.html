About the App

Full-time App for iOS is a beautifully designed and focused job listing app for designers and developers.

I designed this app initially for a friend of mine when he was visiting me from New York. I wanted a way for him to find work easier and quicker without the noise of other job postings. I use Authentic Jobs because it had the most tailored jobs for the web industry.
